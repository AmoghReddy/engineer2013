Description

Metamagic is an exhibition of fascinating experiments designed to amaze even the most skeptical. From Shape memory alloys to smoke bombs to walking on fluids, these dazzling displays will showcase the amazing nature of materials.
- Major demonstrations – Shape Memory Alloy, Superconductor
- Others – Sugar bomb, Instant Ice, Mega Bubble, Elephant Toothpaste, Walking on Water, Chemical Squid, Smoke Bomb, Dry Sand.
